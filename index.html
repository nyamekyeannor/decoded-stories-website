About the host

Hey, I’m Nyamekye

Hey, I’m Nyamekye. I’ve always been the kind of person who’s curious about people, who loves asking the questions nobody else asks. Storytelling never dies, and I built this whole show around one belief: People are so much more interesting than their job titles.

I started Decoded Stories because people once sat with me and shared their stories, and it changed how I saw what was possible for my own life.

By the end of most conversations, guests tell me the same thing: It felt less like an interview and more like a free therapy session. I’ll take it. We’re decoding your story, and apparently, healing a little along the way too.

We’re never just one thing. I’m here to decode the rest.

Ready to be decoded? I want to hear it.
